Earth Sagas began as a video game idea that grew into the beginning of a novel project that's currently in progress. Earth Sagas explores an oddly hopeful post-apocalytic world where characters relearn farming after all flora went dormant for several centuries. This first chapter is a quiet and contemplative introduction full of atmosphere and imagery.
